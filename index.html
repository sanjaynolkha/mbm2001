Our Journey

From Exam Halls to Life's Greatest Moments

Twenty-five years ago, we walked through the gates of MBM Engineering College as curious, ambitious young minds. Today, we return — as engineers, entrepreneurs, parents, dreamers, and most importantly, lifelong friends.

It was 2001 — the year we stepped into the corridors of MBM, armed with textbooks and boundless energy. We pulled all-nighters before exams, argued over cricket scores, and somehow found time to forge friendships that no algorithm could have predicted.

"The best engineering we ever did wasn't in the lab — it was building friendships that have lasted 25 years."

Now, a quarter-century later, we gather again — not as students rushing to class, but as accomplished professionals who have shaped industries, raised families, and lived full, remarkable lives. This reunion is our moment to pause, reflect, and celebrate together.

Join us at the Laria Resort, Jodhpur — the Blue City that nurtured us — as we raise a toast to 25 extraordinary years of the Class of 2001.
